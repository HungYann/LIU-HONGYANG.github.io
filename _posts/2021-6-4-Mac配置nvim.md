添加neovim:

https://github.com/neovim/neovim/wiki/Installing-Neovim

```shell
curl -LO https://github.com/neovim/neovim/releases/download/nightly/nvim-macos.tar.gz
tar xzf nvim-macos.tar.gz
./nvim-osx64/bin/nvim
```



配置环境

```shell
nvim ~/.bash_profile
```
